Team trees is a project dedicated to changing to trejectory of the world in a positive direction. The intiative was founded and inspired by a famous infuencer, who saw a need for change. Team trees has partnered up with a non-profit organization called arbor day to lead in this eco-frienly intiative. Therefore we aim to plant 20 Million Tree's, to boost oxygen levels and reduce the carbon footprint human innovation is causing. We can all contibute to this goal by plannting one tree at a time. A special mention to Arbor Day who have given tremendous support in achieving this ground breaking goal. Together we can make a change, which side will you be on?
