Team Trees is a project dedicated to changing the trajectory of the world in a positive direction. The initiative was founded and inspired by a famous influencer, who saw a need for change. Team Trees has partnered up with a non-profit organization called Arbor Day to lead in this eco-friendly initiative. As we all know, global warming has become one of the highest threats to the earth and its inhabitants. Therefore, we aim to plant 20 million trees to boost oxygen levels and reduce the carbon footprint human innovation is causing. We can all contribute to this goal by planting one tree at a time. Special thanks to Arbor Day for their tremendous support in achieving this groundbreaking goal. Together, we can make a change. Which side will you be on?
